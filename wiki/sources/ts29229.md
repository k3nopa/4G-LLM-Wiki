**Full title:** Cx and Dx interfaces based on the Diameter protocol; Protocol details

**Companion spec:** 3GPP TS 29.228 (Cx/Dx signalling flows and message contents)

---

## Scope

Defines the Diameter protocol for:
- **Cx interface:** I-CSCF / S-CSCF ↔ HSS
- **Dx interface:** I-CSCF / S-CSCF ↔ SLF (Subscriber Locator Function)

Specifies: Application-ID, transport, session mode, routing, 6 command pairs (12 messages), result codes, and 71 AVP definitions.
